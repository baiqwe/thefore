# The Forge (Roblox) Combat Guide: Ranged Weapons & Tactical Strategy

**Why get hit when you can kill from a distance?**

In **The Forge on Roblox**, most players grab a Greatsword and swing wildly. They take damage, burn potions, and die often.

I prefer a smarter approach: **Ranged Combat**.

Whether you choose the precision of a **Bow** or the raw power of a **Staff**, mastering ranged weapons allows you to solo content that melee players can't touch.

---

## Bow vs. Staff: Choosing Your Weapon

Which ranged tool is right for you?

### Weapon Comparison Table

| Feature | Longbow | Magic Staff |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Damage Type** | Physical (Piercing) | Elemental (Magic) |
| **Attack Speed** | Fast | Slow |
| **Range** | Very Long | Medium |
| **Ammo Cost** | Arrows (Gold) | Mana (Regen) |
| **Best For** | Single Target DPS | Area of Effect (AoE) |

**My Pick:** I use the **Longbow** for bosses and the **Staff** for farming groups of weak mobs.

---

## The Art of Kiting

"Kiting" is the essential skill for ranged players. It means moving while attacking to keep enemies out of reach.

### How to Kite Effectively

1. **Shoot:** Fire an attack to draw aggro.
2. **Move:** Immediately run backward or sideways.
3. **Repeat:** Stop briefly to fire again, then keep moving.

> **Pro Tip:** Use the terrain. Jump onto rocks or ledges where melee mobs can't reach you. This is how I farmed the [Forgotten Kingdom](/blog/portal-adventure-forgotten-kingdom) at level 20.

---

## Hybrid Strategies: The "Switch" Tech

You don't have to stick to one weapon. The best players use **Hybrid Builds**.

**The Strategy:**
1. Start with a **Bow** to deal damage from afar.
2. As the enemy closes in, switch to a **Katana** or **Greatsword**.
3. Finish them off with melee combos.

This maximizes DPS (Damage Per Second) and minimizes damage taken.

## Economics of Ranged Combat

Ranged combat isn't free. You need to manage your costs.

### Arrow Costs vs. Potion Savings

| Cost Type | Melee Build | Ranged Build |
|-----------|-------------|--------------|
| **Ammo** | 0 Gold | 2,000 Gold/Hour |
| **Potions** | 5,000 Gold/Hour | 500 Gold/Hour |
| **Repairs** | High (Armor breaks) | Low (Don't get hit) |
| **Net Cost** | **High** | **Low** |

**Verdict:** Ranged builds are actually *cheaper* to run because you save massive amounts on health potions and armor repairs.

## Best Races for Ranged Builds

Your [race choice](/blog/quest-for-dragonborn-bloodline) matters for ranged efficiency.

1. **Elf:** The undisputed king of bows.
   - Passive: +15% Bow Damage
   - Passive: +10% Movement Speed (Essential for kiting)

2. **Fae:** Best for magic users.
   - Passive: +20% Mana Regen
   - Passive: Magic damage bonus

3. **Human:** Decent all-rounder if you play Hybrid.

---

## Advanced Tactics: Elemental Arrows

Later in the game, you can craft **Elemental Arrows**.

- **Fire Arrows:** Burn damage over time. Great for high-HP bosses.
- **Ice Arrows:** Slows enemies by 30%. Makes kiting incredibly easy.
- **Explosive Arrows:** AoE damage. Expensive but clears groups fast.

I save Ice Arrows for [Dungeon Bosses](/blog/dungeon-hidden-bosses-guide). The slow effect is a lifesaver.
