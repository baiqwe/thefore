# The Forge (Roblox) Race Guide: Dragonborn Rerolls & Tier List

**Want the perfect race in The Forge?** This complete guide covers the race system, reroll strategies, and which races actually matter.

In **The Forge on Roblox**, your race determines your starting stats and abilities. After 250+ hours testing every race, I'll share what actually works versus what sounds good on paper.

If [forging](/blog/my-blacksmith-journey-begins) is the soul, then races are your talent foundation.

## Understanding The Race System

When I started, I randomly got **Human** - the most common race. Balanced but boring.

After researching community guides, I learned about rare races with under 5% spawn rates.

| Race | Rarity | Key Bonuses | Best Playstyle |
|------|--------|-------------|----------------|
| **Dragonborn** | 2% | +25% Damage, +20% Health | Combat focus |
| **Undead** | 3% | +30% Defense, Rare death immunity | Survivability |

That Dragonborn damage boost? Game-changing for [combat builds](/blog/weapon-mastery-katana-greatsword).

## How to Reroll Your Race

The race reroll system uses two currencies: free codes and gold.

### Getting Free Rerolls

Community creators release **redeem codes** with free attempts:

- Check YouTube every 2-3 days
- Join official Discord for announcements
- Follow Reddit r/TheForgeRoblox
- Codes expire in 7-14 days

I bookmark code aggregator sites and check daily.

### Reroll Cost Breakdown

| Reroll Number | Gold Cost | Total Spent |
|---------------|-----------|-------------|
| 1-5 (Free codes) | 0 | 0 |
| 6-10 | 10,000 each | 50,000 |
| 11-20 | 25,000 each | 300,000 |
| 21+ | 50,000 each | Very expensive |

**Strategy:** Save gold through [armor crafting](/blog/heavy-armor-crafting-for-profit) before major reroll sessions.

## Optimal Reroll Strategy

After 50+ reroll sessions, here's what works:

### Rule 1: Don't Reroll Below Rare

If you get Elf or Dwarf (15-18% rates), **keep them**. Both excel at economy and provide immediate benefits.

**Exception:** Pure combat players should reroll for Dragonborn or Undead.

### Rule 2: Set a Budget

**Recommended budgets:**
- Beginners: 100k gold max (20 attempts)
- Intermediate: 300k gold (earn through [trading](/blog/advanced-trading-secrets) first)
- Advanced: Unlimited (you're rich enough)

### Rule 3: Understand Probability

With 2% Dragonborn rate:
- 20 rerolls = 33% chance
- 50 rerolls = 64% chance  
- 100 rerolls = 87% chance

Don't expect instant results. Manage expectations.

## Common Reroll Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Rerolling Too Early

Players with 20k gold spend everything on rerolls. Don't.

That gold funds [equipment upgrades](/blog/enhancement-and-rune-experiments) and materials. Delay rerolls until level 25+.

### Mistake 2: Chasing Perfection

I spent 1,500 gems chasing perfect rolls. The difference between perfect and average of same race? About 3% actual impact.

Not worth obsessing over.

### Mistake 3: Ignoring Racial Abilities

I picked a race for stats and forgot about the special ability for 30 hours. When I finally used it strategically, it didn't fit my playstyle at all.

Test abilities before committing long-term.

## Is Race Important?

**Short answer:** Yes, but less than you think.

Skilled Human players destroy unskilled Dragonborn players through superior combat timing and resource management.
